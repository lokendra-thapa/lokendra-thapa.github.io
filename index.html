About Me

An enthusiastic and dedicated Agricultural Engineering graduate from Tribhuvan University, Nepal, with a strong academic foundation in crop science, soil and water management, GIS and remote sensing, farm mechanization, and sustainable agriculture.

I am passionate about exploring digital agriculture and precision farming technologies to make a real impact at the farmers' level. Always open to opportunities to gain hands-on experience, enhance practical understanding of modern agricultural systems, and contribute to innovative solutions that improve farm productivity, resource efficiency, and rural livelihoods.

Professional Focus

My work focuses on leveraging technology to solve agricultural challenges in Nepal. I specialize in:

  • Digital Agriculture: Developing agro-advisory systems and data-driven solutions
  • Precision Farming: Implementing GIS and remote sensing for smart agriculture
  • Irrigation Systems: Designing efficient greenhouse, drip, and sprinkler systems
  • Data Analytics: Market analysis and forecasting for agricultural decision-making

Vision

I am eager to learn from professionals, apply technical knowledge in real-world settings, and grow as a future agricultural innovator. My goal is to bridge the gap between advanced agricultural technology and practical implementation at the farmer's level in Nepal.

Leadership & Community

Beyond technical work, I have served as Vice Secretary of the Nepal Agricultural Engineering Students' Society (NAESS) and contributed to organizing technical events like DELTA 2.0, Nepal's first technical festival.
